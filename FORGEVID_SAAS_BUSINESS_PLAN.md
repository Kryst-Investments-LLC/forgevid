# 💼 ForgeVid SaaS Business Plan & Financial Projections

## 📋 Executive Summary

**ForgeVid** is an AI-powered video creation platform that enables businesses to create professional marketing videos in minutes instead of weeks, at 1/100th the cost of traditional agencies.

**Market Opportunity:** $13.6B video production market  
**Target:** Small businesses, marketing agencies, content creators  
**Pricing:** $29-$199/month subscription  
**Competitive Advantage:** AI script generation + intelligent scene matching  

---

## 🎯 Product Overview

### **What ForgeVid Does:**

1. **AI Script Generation** (GPT-4)
   - Generates professional video scripts from prompts
   - Scene-by-scene breakdowns with visual descriptions
   - Camera angles, transitions, music suggestions

2. **Intelligent Stock Footage Compilation**
   - Searches Pexels for scene-specific footage
   - Downloads HD clips matching each scene
   - Trims to exact durations
   - Assembles with professional transitions

3. **Automated Video Production**
   - 1080p HD output
   - 15-300 second videos
   - Professional quality in 3-5 minutes
   - Downloadable MP4 files

### **Key Features:**
- ✅ Multi-language support (10 languages)
- ✅ Scene-by-scene matching (unique!)
- ✅ HD quality output (1080p)
- ✅ Unlimited video generation
- ✅ Professional dashboard
- ✅ Template library
- ✅ Media management
