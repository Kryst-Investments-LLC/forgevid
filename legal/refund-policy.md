This Policy is part of the Terms of Service and explains billing, cancellation, and refunds for
**[ForgeVid, Inc.]**

## 1. Subscriptions & auto-renewal
- Paid plans are billed **in advance**, per period (monthly unless stated), and **renew automatically**
  at the then-current price until you cancel. We disclose the recurring amount and interval, and obtain
  your consent, at checkout.
- We send a **receipt for each charge** and, where required, a **renewal reminder** before it bills.

## 2. Cancellation
- **You may cancel anytime from Billing settings — it's as simple as signing up.** Cancellation stops
  the **next** renewal; your plan stays active until the end of the current paid period, then does not
  renew.
- We do **not** pro-rate or refund the unused part of a period on cancellation, except as stated below
  or required by law.

## 3. Refunds
Because the Service delivers **digital goods that are generated and consumed immediately** (compute,
AI, and render credits), **fees are generally non-refundable.** However:
- **First-time subscribers:** if you're unhappy, contact us within **[7/14] days** of your **first**
  paid charge and we may, at our discretion, refund that first charge **if you have used little or none
  of your plan's video quota**.
- **Duplicate or accidental charges**, or charges after a valid cancellation, will be **refunded**.
- **Statutory rights preserved:** nothing here limits non-waivable rights you may have under consumer
  law (e.g., an EU/UK **14-day right of withdrawal** for consumers — note this may not apply once you
  have started generating videos and consented to immediate performance).
- **Renewals** are generally non-refundable; cancel before a renewal date to avoid the next charge.

## 4. Failed or defective renders
If a video **fails to render due to a fault on our side**, we will **re-render it or credit the video
back to your quota** — this is your exclusive remedy for a failed render, and it is not a cash refund.
Renders that fail because of your inputs (e.g., unreachable feed URLs, missing/invalid photos, content
that violates the Acceptable Use Policy) are your responsibility.

## 5. Free trials & credits
Any free trial converts to a paid subscription unless you cancel before it ends. Promotional credits
have no cash value and expire as stated when granted.

## 6. Price changes
We give advance notice of price increases (at least **30 days**, or as required by law); they apply at
your next renewal, and you may cancel beforehand.

## 7. Chargebacks
If you believe a charge is wrong, **please contact us first at [billing@forgevid.com]** — we usually
resolve it faster than a bank dispute. Filing a chargeback without contacting us may result in
suspension pending resolution.

## 8. How to request a refund
Email **[billing@forgevid.com]** from your account email with your account, the charge date, and the
reason. We aim to respond within **[5] business days**.
