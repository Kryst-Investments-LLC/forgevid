# Compliance Checklist

- [ ] GDPR ([GDPR Policy](docs/policies/gdpr-policy.md))
- [ ] SOC2 ([SOC2 Controls](docs/policies/soc2-controls.md))
- [ ] PCI-DSS ([PCI Policy](docs/policies/pci-policy.md))
- [ ] HIPAA ([HIPAA Policy](docs/policies/hipaa-policy.md))
- [ ] Regular security audits ([Security Audit Log](docs/security-audit-log.md))
- [ ] Data retention policy ([Retention Policy](docs/policies/data-retention.md))
- [ ] Incident response plan ([Incident Response](docs/disaster-recovery-runbook.md))

## Instructions
- For each item, link to the policy and document how compliance is achieved.
- Review and update policies quarterly.
- Maintain audit logs and evidence in `docs/security-audit-log.md`.
- Ensure all staff are trained on compliance procedures.
